### Short URL Services Used
All services used are reputable and widely trusted:

- **TinyURL:** Established 2002, 20+ years of service
- **is.gd:** Privacy-focused, no tracking cookies
- **v.gd:** Sister service to is.gd, same privacy policy

### Important Notes
1. **No tracking by default:** is.gd and v.gd don't track users
2. **Permanent links:** These short URLs won't expire
3. **Cannot be edited:** Once created, destination cannot be changed
4. **Public access:** Anyone with the link can access
5. **Spam filter safe:** All services are whitelisted by major email providers
